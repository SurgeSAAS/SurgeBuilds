// Our Story

About Us

Founded nearly two years ago by two young entrepreneurs, Dalton and Alex, Platinum Detailing has grown from a passionate side hustle into one of Massachusetts' most trusted mobile detailing services. Now at just 18 years old, we've detailed dozens and dozens of vehicles, from daily drivers to luxury showpieces — each one treated with unmatched care, precision, and professionalism.

Our mission is simple: Deliver showroom-quality results with the convenience of coming straight to your doorstep. Whether it's a full interior transformation or a complete exterior refresh, our detail packages are designed to elevate your ride and restore that "like-new" feeling.

We're fully mobile and self-contained — with our own water and power supply, we can detail your car anywhere: home, work, or even your job site. Busy day? No problem. We just need your car and a spot to park — we'll handle the rest.

We're proud to be backed by a 5-star Google rating, and even prouder to serve the community that believed in us from the start.

Your car, our passion — experience the Platinum difference.
